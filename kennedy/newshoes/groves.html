The Groves Of Donaghmore
   
Credits: R. J. MacNeil; traditional lyrics; arranged by Nuala Kennedy, Claire Mann, Julian Sutton & Marc Clement
   
Appears On: The New Shoes
   
Language: English

And I'm going home, no more to roam from the Groves of Donaghmore
