Air Do Bhanais
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Maggie MacInnes & Marie Fielding
Appears On : Eilean Mara
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " Bidh Clann Ulaidh Air Do Bhanais " on Ishbel MacAskill's album Essentially Ishbel

Lyrics : English Translation :
Bidh Clann Ulaidh luaidh 's a lurain The Clans of Ulster, my darling, my treasure
Bidh Clann Ulaidh air do bhanais The Clans of Ulster will be at your wedding
Bidh Clann Ulaidh luaidh 's a lurain The Clans of Ulster, my darling, my treasure
Deanamh an danns' air do bhanais Will be dancing at your wedding
Sèist : Chorus (after each verse) :
Bidh clann a righ, bidh clann a righ The family of the king, the family of the king
Bidh clann a righ air do bhanais The family of the king will be at your wedding
Bidh clann a righ seinnear a phiob The family of the king, pipes will be played
Olar a fion air do bhanais Wine will be drunk at your wedding

Bidh Clann Amhlaidh air do bhanais The Clan MacAulay will be at your wedding
