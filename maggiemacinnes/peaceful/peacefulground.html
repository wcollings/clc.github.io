Peaceful Ground
   
Credits : Maggie MacInnes
   
Appears On : Peaceful Ground
   
Language : English
   

Lyrics :

You reached out to me in the dark
Your words spoke to my soul
The sea then seemed to go calm
And I struggled to the shore

Chorus :
On my mind you'll always be
In my heart for no one to see
And in you I have found
The perfect peaceful ground

Sometimes confusion and pain
Slip out from behind your eyes
I see you drifting away
And I want to hold you tight

(Chorus)

If you stop running so fast
Slow down and feel the sun
Shining down on your face
Perhaps you'll follow my path

In that place there is peace
Joy and complete calm
The rocks, the sea and the sand
Wild flowers between your toes

(Chorus 2x)
