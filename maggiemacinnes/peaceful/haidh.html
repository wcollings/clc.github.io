Haidh O
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Maggie MacInnes & Graeme Hughes
   
Appears On: Peaceful Ground
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Haghaidh O, Haghaidh O" on Mackenzie's album Camhanach
"Catharsis" on Tannas' album Suilean Dubh
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Sèist: Chorus (after each verse):
Haidh o haidh o ho Haidh o haidh o ho
Haidh o a hee hu bho ho Haidh o a hee hu bho ho
Haidh o haidh o ho Haidh o haidh o ho
   
An cuala sibh mun mhaideann chèitich? Did you hear about the young maiden?
   
Air an tug Niall Òg an' Èiginn She was taken by young Neil
   
Air taobh beinne ri latha grèineadh At the side of a hill on a sunny day
   
'S truagh a rìgh nach b'e mi fhèin i It's a pity it wasn't me
   
'S mi nach deanadh glaodh na èughach I wouldn't have screamed or shouted
   
Cha strachdainn brollach a lèineadh I wouldn't have torn his shirt
   
Na strachdadh gu fuaghlainn fhèin i If it tore I'd sew it myself
   
Le snàthaid bhig 's le snàth glè gheal With fine pure white thread
   
Nighinn ann a fuaran slèibh i And I'd wash it in the stream
   
Thiormaichinn air bhàrr nan geug i And dry it on the bushes
