I Wish My Love Was A Red Red Rose
Credits : n/a
Appears On : Live In Concert
Language : English
Other Versions : " I Wish My Love Was A Red Red Rose " on Altan's album Runaway Sunday
" I Wish My Love Was A Red Red Rose " on Méav Ní Mhaolchatha's album Méav

Lyrics :

I wish my love was a red, red rose growing in yon garden fair
And me to be the gardener, of her I would take care
There's not a month throughout the year, but my love I would renew
I'd garnish her with flowers fine, sweet William, Thyme and Rue

I wish I was a butterfly, I'd light on my love's breast
Or if I was a blue cuckoo, I'd sing my love to rest
Or if I was a nightingale, I'd sing the daylight clear
I'd sit and sing for you, Molly, for once I loved you dear

I wish I was in Dublin and seated on the grass
In my right hand, a jug of punch, and on my knee, a lass
I'd call for liquor freely and I'd pay before I'd go
And I'd roll her in me arms, let the wind blow high or low
