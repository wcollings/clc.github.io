Chunna Mise Mo Leannan

Appears On : Mary Ann Kennedy and Na Seòid
Language : Gàidhlig
Lyrics: English Translation:
Gura mise tha fo mhulad air an tulaich, 's mòr m' eislean I am sad on the hillock, great is my sorrow
Sèist : Chorus (after every line):
Ho ro ho hi hoireannan, Ho ro ho hi hoireannan,
horo chall eile, horo chall eile,
ho ro ho hi hoireannan ho ro ho hi hoireannan
Chunna mise mo leannan 's cha do th'aithnich e fhèin mi I saw my love but he did not even recognise me
Cha d'fhidir, 's cha d'fharraid, cha do ghabh e bhuam sgeula He took no notice, did not ask, did not listen to my news
Ach cha ruigeadh e leas e, cha robh mis' an trom dèidh air He need not have, I was not so fond of him
Dol timchioll a' rubha, bha mi dubhach, trom-dheurach Going round the headland, I was depressed and weeping constantly
'S iad mo rùn-sa Clann Dòmhnaill, son an còmhlan nach trèig mi My beloved Clan Donald, they are the band I will not betray
Seun Mhoire san Dòmhnaich, le Clann Dòmhnaill nan geur-lann The blessing of Mary and the Lord’s day, with Clan Donald of the sharpest blades
Luchd nan calpannan troma, chùl dona, cheum eutrom Men of the stout calves, brown-haired and light of step
Luchd tharraing nam biodag fo chriosan nan fhìlihdh Men who would draw their daggers from beneath the belts of their plaids
Luchd nan claidheamhnan caol geala, chìthte faileas dhiubh làth grèine Men of the slender white swords, glinting in the summer’s sunlight
Luchd nam boghannan iubhair, dh'fhuilingeadh lùbadh 's nach leumadh Men of the yew longbows, that would bend without slipping its bowstring
'S nan gunnaichean dubha, chuireadh smùid air na slèibhte And the black guns that would send smoke rising from the slopes
