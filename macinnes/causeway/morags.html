Morag's Na Horo Gheallaidh
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Mairi MacInnes
   
Appears On: Causeway
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Mhorag's Na Horo Gheallaidh" on Clannad's album Fuaim
