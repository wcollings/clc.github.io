Mo Chridhe Trom's Duilich Leam
   
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Mairi MacInnes & Phil Cunningham
   
Appears On : Causeway
   
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions : " Mo Chridhe Trom 'S Dùilich Leam " on Fiona Mackenzie & Arthur Cormack's album Seinn! O Ho Ro Seinn!
" Mo Chridhe Trom " on Anna Murray's album Out Of The Blue
