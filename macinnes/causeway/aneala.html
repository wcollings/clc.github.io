An Eala Bhàn
Credits : Domhnall Ruadh Choruna; arranged by Mairi MacInnes & Phil Cunningham
Appears On : Causeway
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " An Eala Bhan " on Capercaillie's album Cascade
