Month Of July
   
Credits: Martin Swan
   
Appears On: Seafaring Man
   
Language: English
   

Lyrics:

In my dream I see her smiling
Though the picture I keep is torn and old
Those were violent days, two worlds colliding
The best and the worst has been and gone

Chorus:
And all the leaves are turning brown
Yet I can find no reason
To tear myself from Puget Sound
And see the world again

The month of July she sent a postcard
Though she wrote not one word, I know her style
It's the winter she fears and looking forward
But my sheltering days have been and gone

(Chorus...)
