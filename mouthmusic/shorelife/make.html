Make It Real
   
Credits: Quee MacArthur, Martin Swan & Jackie Joyce
   
Appears On: Shorelife
   
Language: English & Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Is my dream impossible asylum?
Has my love nothing to rely on?
Will my world crumble to the ground?
Is it true? Have I lost what I just found?
If a dream were real, would it be so pure?
The sweetest taste, the strongest lure
The incense of the richest spice
Will my dream become my vice?

Is this the way I'll always feel?
