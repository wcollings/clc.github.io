Time
Credits : Jackie Joyce & Martin Swan
Appears On : Shorelife
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic) & English

Lyrics : English Translation :
Leis an t-struth bhor... Carried by the force of the current...
Like the cold wind blowing in the wintertime

While they wait in line for their time
Like the snow in winter waiting bright night
And darkness heralding the morning light
While they wait in line for their time
As the wild flowers awaken with one drop of life
Will ne'er bloom lest they receive that drop of life
While they wait in line for their time
Like the gold elms in autumn, they shed their leaves
When springtime befalls us they are retrieved
While they wait in line for their time
While time rushes by us with so much impatience
Time washes over us and yet another day is gone
The day is gone, to time that never will obey

While we wait in line for all time
While time rushes by us with so much impatience
Time washes over us and yet another day is gone
The day is gone, we live and we die
