Igitur Servus
Credits : Arranged by Michael McGlynn
Appears On : Cynara
Language : Latin

Lyrics : English Translation :
Igitur servus Christi serviens In the course of his service as a servant of Christ
Malignorum demonum servos The servants of wicked demons
Quorum multos peccatorum a vinculis solvit Many of whom he released from the chains of their sins
Malignorum demonum Wicked demons
Et angelice liberatos He freed like an angel
Felices consociavit And brought over to his side in joy
