Incantations
   
Credits: Michael McGlynn
   
Appears On: Cynara
   
Language: Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
'S Tusa an dámh, 's Tusa an éan You are the stag, You are the bird
'S Tusa an t-iasc, aililú You are the fish, alleluia
'S Tusa an ghaoth, 's Tusa an fuacht You are the wind, You are the cold
'S Tusa an mhuir, aililú You are the sea, alleluia
'S Tusa an ghrian, 's Tusa an réalt You are the sun, You are the star
'S Tusa an spéir, aililú You are the sky, alleluia
Aililú mo Íosa, aililú mo chroí Alleluia my Jesus, alleluia my heart
Aililú mo Thiarna, aililú mo Chríost Alleluia my Lord, alleluia my Christ
