Silent Night
   
Credits: Music by Franz Gruber; lyrics by Josef Mohr; arranged by Michael McGlynn
   
Appears On: Songs For A Celtic Christmas; Winter Songs
   
Language: English
   
Other Versions: "Oíche Chúin" on Moya Brennan's album An Irish Christmas
"Oíche Chiúin" on Enya's single Only If...
"Silent Night" on Katie McMahon's album Celtic Christmas
"Silent Night" on Méav Ní Mhaolchatha's album A Celtic Journey
   

Lyrics:

Silent night, holy night
All is calm, all is bright
'Round yon virgin mother and child
Holy infant so tender and mild
Sleep in heavenly peace
Sleep in heavenly peace
(2x)
