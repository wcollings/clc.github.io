Ave Generosa
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Máire Lang
   
Appears On: Omnis (Irish Release); Omnis (U.S. Release)
   
Language: Latin
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Ave, generosa Hail, girl of nobility
Gloriosa et intacta puella Unstained and shining
Tu pupilla castitatis You pupil in the eye of chastity
Tu materia sanctitatis You embodiment of holiness
Que Deo placuit Pleasing unto God
Ave... Hail...
