Language : Old English
   

Lyrics : Translation :
   
Maid in the moor lay sevenyhtes fulle Maid in the moor lay seven nights full

Wat was hire mete? What was her meat?
The primerole and the violet The primrose and the violet
Wat was hire drynk? What was her drink?
The colde water of the welle-sprink The cold water of the well-spring
Wat was hire bour? What was her bed?
The rede rose and the lili-flour The red rose and the lily flower
