Bí Liom
Credits : Moya Brennan & Ross Cullum
Appears On : Two Horizons
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
Bí liom a stór mo chroísa Be with me forever, my love
Ná scaoil mé go deo Don't ever let go of me
Bí liom is tabhairt domh misneach To give me courage
Gan eagla orm sa cheo I will not be afraid through the mist
Bí liom nach fada 'n oíche I am alone through this long night but
Gan tú ag mo thaobh Without you at my side
Suílfidh mé amach ar imeall I will walk to the edge
Ag cuartú ceol binn úr In search of a new song
Bí liom ar lorg síocháin Be with me to find the peace
Is saoirse fadó And freedom of long ago
Dúirt tú liom: éist fuaim an chláirseach You told me to listen for the harp
Sin é an ceol binn úr This is where I will find the new song
