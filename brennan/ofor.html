O For A Closer Walk
Credits : Keith Getty
Appears On : In Christ Alone
Language : English & Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
Oh for a closer walk with God
A calm and heavenly frame
A light that shines upon the road
Leading to the Lamb
Cá bhfuil na beannachtaí __ Where is the blessedness I knew
Nuair a tháinig mé mo Dhia? When I once saw the Lord?
Cá bhfuil m'anam __ Where is the soul-refreshing view
Beo i mo shaoil Living in His word?
Chorus :
A light to be my guide
The Father's presence at my side
In Your will my rest I find
Oh for a closer walk with God
Leading to the Lamb
So shall my walk be close with God
With all the hopes made new
So purer light shall mark the road
Leading to the Lamb
(Chorus 2x)
