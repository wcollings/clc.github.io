Rinne Tú
   
Credits: Máire Brennan & Tim Jarvis
   
Appears On: Whisper To The Wild Water
   
Language: Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Seolaigh mé ar uisce beo I will sail on living water
Éist le fuaim an ghaoth Listen to the wind
Neartaigh ar ár dturas mé Protect me on my journey
Tusa liom go deo Stay with me forever

Rinne Tú na sléibhe You made the mountains
Thug Tú na blátha dúinn You gave us flowers
Thug Tú Tú fhéin You gave Yourself
