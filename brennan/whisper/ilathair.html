I Láthair Dé
   
Credits: Máire Brennan, Tim Jarvis & Denis Woods
   
Appears On: Whisper To The Wild Water
   
Language: Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Taispeáin anois mo bhealach Show me the way
Tabhair aire domsa a Thiarna __ oh Lord
Gach lá 's oiche Every day and night
Éist a Dhia le mo ghuí Listen to my plea
   
Dóchas thug Tú domh istigh You gave me hope
D'éist Tú liom nuair a bhí mé shíos When I was feeling down
Is Tú tá dílis agus trócaireach You are true and full of compassion
Líon mé le do Spiorad do deo Forever fill me with Your Spirit
Seinnfidh mé fá do ghlóire I will sing of Your glory
'S na n-aingil i láthair Dé And the angels in God's presence
   
Treoraigh anois mo shaol sa In this world guide me
Líon suas mo chroí le grá mór Fill my heart with great love
Tusa an Rí You are the King
Is Tú a rinne neamh agus talamh You made heaven and earth
   
Chuir Tú siocháin in mo chroí You put peace in my heart
Gheal Tú aoibhneas in mo shaol You brought happiness to my life
Seinnfidh mé an cláirseach duitse I will play the harp
Is molfaidh mé Thú le mo bheo To praise you as long as I live
