Gone Are The Days
Credits : Moya Brennan, Fionán De Barra & Paul Byrne
Appears On : Signature
Language : English & Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
When I was younger then
Found sweeter moments
No worries or cost
Not guilty or lost
Never afraid to fly
Was never afraid to cry
Fada, fada go banú an lae... Long, long until daybreak...
