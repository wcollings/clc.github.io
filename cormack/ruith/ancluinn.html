An Cluinn Thu Mi Mo Nighean Donn
   
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Arthur Cormack & Blair Douglas
   
Appears On : Ruith Na Gaoith
   
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics : English Translation :
   
Sèist : Chorus (after each verse) :
An cluinn thu mi, mo nighean donn? Will you listen to me, my brown-haired girl?
Dèan éisd is thoir an aire dhomh Listen, and pay attention to me
Tha móran dhaoin' 's a' bharail seo There are many people of this opinion
Gur òg an leannan domhs' thu That you are too young a love for me
   
Is ged bu liath mo chiabhagan And although my hair is gray
Na cuireadh sin fo iargain thu Don't let this distress you
Cha'n 'eil mi sean am bliadhnachan I am not old in years
'S bu mhiann leam bhith 'nad chòir-sa And I long to be with you
   
Thar chuantan mór gun seòlainn-sa Over wide oceans I will sail
Is bheirinn dhachaidh stòras dhuit And I will take home riches to you
Is bhitheadh tu gu dòigheil leam And you would be happy with me
Ri d' bheò ma tha thu deònach For the rest of your life, if you're willing
   
Nis sguiridh mi bhith 'm sheòladair Now I will stop sailing
'S air tìr gu fan mi còmhla riut I will stay with you on dry land
On 's fhad 's a tha mi eòlach ort For as far as I know
Bu bhòicheach leam do dhòighean Your ways are beautiful to me
