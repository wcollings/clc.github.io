Thoir Dhomh Do Lamh
   
Credits: a) Thoir Dhomh Do Lamh - Sandra MacKay
b) Ruidhleadh Na Coilich Dhubha - traditional; arranged by Tannas
   
Appears On: Suilean Dubh
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
