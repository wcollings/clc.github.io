Breisleach
Credits : Donald Shaw; lyrics by Aonghas MacNeacail; arranged by James Graham, James Ross & Mary Ann Kennedy

Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " Breisleach " on Capercaillie's album Delirium
