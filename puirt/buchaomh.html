Bu chaomh leam bhith fuireach

Bu chaomh leam bhith fuireach an Uibhist bheag lurach I would like to live in beautiful, small Uist
Bu chaomh leam bhith fuireach an Uibhist bheag m' òig I would like to live in small Uist of my youth
Bu chaomh leam bhith fuireach an Uibhist bheag lurach I would like to live in beautiful, small Uist
'S i tìr nan glas-mhullach, a' mhurain 's an eòrn It's the land of the gray-tops, maram grass and barley
