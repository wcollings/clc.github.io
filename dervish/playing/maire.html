Maire Mhor
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Dervish
   
Appears On: Playing With Fire; Live In Palma
   
Language: Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Faoitín" on Maighread & Tríona Ní Dhomhnaill's album Idir An Dá Sholas
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Ta bean a'msa in Arainn Mhor I have a wife in Aranmore
Is bean eile in Inis Bearachain And another wife in Inis Bearachain
Ach mo ghra i Maire Mhor But my love is Máire Mhór
Is i an bhean is fearr ar fad acu She is the best woman __
