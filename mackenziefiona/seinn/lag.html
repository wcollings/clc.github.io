Lag Nan Cruachan
Credits : Captain A. Lamont
Appears On : Seinn! O Ho Ro Seinn!
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
Ma thèid thu Lag nan Cruachan Should you go to Lag nan Cruachan
Dèan innsidh dhaibh mur cual' iad e Do tell them unless they have heard
Gur ann air moch Diluain, le ghaoth That it was early on Monday morning
Thuath a rinn sinn sèoladh With a north wind we sailed
Nuair dh'fhag sinn a mach Grianaig When we left from Greenock
Bha ceò is fiath nan ian againn We had mist and calm weather
'S ged chuir sinn h-uile stiall oirre And although every stitch of sail was hoisted
Cha dèanadh i dhuinn crònan She would hardly move for us
'N sin fhuair sinn mar a b'àill leinn i Later it was as we would wish it
Is chroch sinn na siùil arda rithe And we hoisted the top-sails
Nuair chuimhnich mi mo mhàthair When I remembered my mother
Bhith ga fàgail bha mi brònach Leaving her, I was sad
Diardaoin bha latha b'eibhinn ann On Thursday we had a good day
Ri taobh an fhearainn Eireannaich Beside the Irish land
'S i again mar a dh'fheumamaid And sufficient wind for us
Tighinn as ar dèidh le sòlas Joyfully following us
