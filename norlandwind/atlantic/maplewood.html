Maplewood And Strawberries
   
Credits : Thomas Loefke; arranged by Kerstin Blodig & Ian Vance Melrose
   
Appears On : Atlantic Driftwood
   
Language : English
   

Lyrics :

Maplewood and strawberries...

Maplewood and strawberries in the luscious scent of May
Streams of warm and curly light, fair hues of an early day
Hundred tender islands in the morning haze at first
Then the voice of clearest water after long, dry days of thirst
