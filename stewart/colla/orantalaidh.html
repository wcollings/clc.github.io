Òran Tàlaidh An Eich-Uisge
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Margaret Stewart & Allan MacDonald
   
Appears On: Colla Mo Rùn
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
O hó bà a leinibh hó Sleep my child
O hó bà a leinibh hà Sleep my child
Bà a leinibh hó bha hó Sleep my child
Hó bà a leinibh hao i hà Sleep my child
   
Hì hó, hó bha hó Hì hó, hó bha hó
Hì hó, hao i hà Hì hó, hao i hà
'S luath dha d' chois thu, hó bha hó Fast of foot you are
'S mór nad each thu, hao i hà Great as a horse you are
   
O hó m'eudail a mac hó My darling son
O hó m'eachan sgèimheach hà Oh my lovely little horse
'S fhad 'n 'n bhail' thu, hò bha hò You are far from the township
Nìtear d'iarraidh, hao i hà You will be sought after
