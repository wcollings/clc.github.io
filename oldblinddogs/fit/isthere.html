Is There For Honest Poverty
   
Credits : Robert Burns; arranged by Old Blind Dogs
   
Appears On : Fit?
   
Language : Scots-English
   
Other Versions : " A Man's A Man For A' That " on Old Blind Dogs' album Play Live

Is there for honest poverty
