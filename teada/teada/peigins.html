Peigín's Peadar
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Téada
Appears On : Téada
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " Peigin Mo Chroi " on Dervish's album Playing With Fire

Lyrics : English Translation :
A Pheigín na gcarad 's a Pheigín mo chroí Peigín, my friend, and Peigín, my heart
Cé hé an fear fada údan sínte leat síos? Who is that tall man stretched alongside you?
