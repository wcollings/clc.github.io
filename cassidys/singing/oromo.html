Óró Mo Bháidín
   
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Na Casaidigh
   
Appears On : Singing From Memory
   
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions : " Hin Barra " on Anúna's album Invocation
" O Row My Little Boat " on Katie McMahon's album Shine
" Óró Mo Bháidín " on Cathie Ryan's album Cathie Ryan
   

Lyrics : English Translation :
   
Crochfaidh mé seolta is rachaidh mé siar I'll set sail and journey west
Óró mo churaichín ó Óró my curaichín ó
'S go hOíche Fhéil' Eoin ní thiocfaidh mé aniar 'Til midsummer night I won't return
Óró mo bháidín Óró my little boat
