Bi-se I Mo Shuil - Part 1
   
Credits: Iona; lyrics adapted by Hugh Brennan
   
Appears On: Journey Into The Morn
   
Language: Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Bí Thusa Mo Shúile" on Máire Brennan's album Whisper To The Wild Water
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Bí-se i mo shúil Be my eyes
A Rí mhór na ndúil Oh King of creation
Líon thusa mo bheatha Fill my life
Mo cheadfaí's mo stuaim With understanding and patience
Bí thusa i m'aigne Will You be in my mind
Gach oíche's gach lá Every night and everyday?
Im chodladh no im dhúiseacht Sleeping or awake
Líon mé le do ghrá Fill me with Your love
   
Bí thusa mo threorú Will You be my guidance
I mbriathar is i mbeart In my words and actions?
Fan thusa go deo liom Stay with me forever
Is coinnigh mé ceart And keep me on the right path
Glac curam mar Athair As my Father take care of me
Is eist le mo ghuí And listen to my prayers
Is tabhair domsa áit And give me a place
Conaí istigh i do chroí To live inside Your heart
