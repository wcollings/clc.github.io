Irish Day
   
Credits: Iona
   
Appears On: Journey Into The Morn
   
Language: English
   

Lyrics:

Sand as white as snow swirls like smoke around my feet
And a sky that turns in a moment from blue to gray
All these things I see on an Irish day

Chorus:
It is here that time has granted
That the light should still burn on
It was here a seed was planted
In the brave heart of an Irish son

Here before my time walked men of faith and truth
In a land that was dark they followed the way
Bringing sweet light on an Irish day

(Chorus 2x)

Here I kneel upon this ground
Love can heal when truth is found
(2x)
