Wisdom
Credits : Iona
Appears On : Journey Into The Morn
Language : English

Lyrics :

You were the very first before the world began
And the oceans rose and the rivers ran
And the water flowed before the mountains and hills were set in place
Before the stars in the sky and the human race
You were there, You were there

Chorus :
Wisdom, teach me what is right
Widsom, come and give me sight
Wisdom, come and be my light
My light

You are the voice of truth that is just and right
A shield to the faithful, a sword for the fight
A path for the wise and those who desire You more than silver or gold
Will find understanding within the soul
For You are there, You are there

(Chorus...)
