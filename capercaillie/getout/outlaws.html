Outlaws
Credits : John Saich
Appears On : Get Out ; Dusk Till Dawn
Language : English

Lyrics :

She stands at the window, proud Mary, bad news
Demands, full credit, and the sheriff's men too
The wife of a fisherman no longer at sea
She can always find him where whiskey flows free
She never called it poverty, the doorstep is clean
'Til cityhall came calling to show what it means
