Tighinn Air A'mhuir Am Fear A Phosas Mi
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Capercaillie
   
Appears On: Nàdurra
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Tha Mulaidh" on Alyth McCormack's album An Iomall
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Is pòsaidh mi, is pòsaidh mi I will marry, I will marry
   
Theid thu null air an fhadhail ni thu sin You will go over the ford, so you will
Thig thu nall air an fhadhail ni thu sin You will go over the ford, so you will
Theid thu null air an fhadhail ni thu gruagach a thadhal You will go over the ford, you'll choose the handsome one
Far na dh'fhàg thu do roghainn ni thu sin Where you have left your chosen one, you will do that
   
Sèist: Chorus:
O tha m'ulaidh air tha m'ulaidh air cha bhith e ris an òl I like him, I like him, he doesn't drink
Tha maighear air tha maighear air cha bhi e ris an òl I like him, I like him, he doesn't drink
Meudail air an t-sùil 's air a mhala th'os a cionn I love his eyebrows and his eyes
Gaòl mo chridhe air a ghille gràdhan air I love him to bits
   
Tighinn air a'mhuir tha fear a phòsas mì Coming on the sea is the man who'll marry me
Tighinn air a'mhuir tha fear a phòsas mì Coming on the sea is the man who'll marry me
Tighinn air a'mhuir tha'n gille buidhe bòidheach Coming on the sea is the blond beautiful lad
Cìobair nan oithisgean is pòsaidh mi I will marry the shepherd of the yearling sheep
