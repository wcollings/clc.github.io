Hoireann O
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Capercaillie
   
Appears On: Nàdurra
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Cronan Bleoghainn" on Karen Matheson's album Downriver
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
'S moch an diugh a rinn mi eirigh Early I arose
