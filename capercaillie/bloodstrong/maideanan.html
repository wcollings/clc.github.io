Maideanan Na h-Airidh
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Capercaillie
   
Appears On: The Blood Is Strong
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Maighdeanan Na H-Airidh" on Capercaillie's album Cascade
