Inexile
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Capercaillie
Appears On : Beautiful Wasteland ; Dusk Till Dawn ; Live In Concert ; The Future Sound Of Gaeldom (compilation)
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " Ille Dhuinn Chaidh Tu'm Dhìth " on Fiona Mackenzie & Arthur Cormack's album Seinn! O Ho Ro Seinn!
