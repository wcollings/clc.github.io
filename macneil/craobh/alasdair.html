Alasdair Mhic Cholla Ghasda
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Flora MacNeil

Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Alasdair Mhic Cholla Ghasda" on Capercaillie's album Sidewaulk
