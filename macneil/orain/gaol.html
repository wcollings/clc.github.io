Gaol Ise Gaol I
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Flora MacNeil
   
Appears On: Orain Floraidh
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Gur E Mis' Tha Gu Tinn" by Maeve MacKinnon on the Gaelic Scotland compilation
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Gaol ise gaol i She's my love
   
Sèist: Chorus (after each verse):
Eo hao o hao o Eo hao o hao o
Hao ri ri o hu o Hao ri ri o hu o

Gaol air Anna ni' n Nill Love for Anna, daughter of Neil
   
Mi dualach mi donn My hair, curly and brown
   
Mi mar chuthaig an coill' Like a cuckoo in a wood
   
Mi ma smeòrach an craoibh Like a thrush on a tree
   
Mi gu biorshuileach binn Keen my eyes, sweet my voice
   
Mi torrach mi trom I am heavy with child
   
Chan ann le balach mo throm But no ordinary lad is my child
   
Ach leis an lasgaire dhonn But by the dark-haired hero
   
Mac fir Bhaile nan Long Son of the Laird of the ships
   
Leis an èireadh na suinn With whom warriors would rise
