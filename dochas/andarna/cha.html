Cha D'fhuair Mi'n Cadal
Credits : n/a
Appears On : An Dàrna Umhail
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
Thog sinn oirnn gu tighinn dhan dùthaich We set a course for the land
'S bha 'n ceò dùmhail air na beannan And a think mist shrouded the mountains
