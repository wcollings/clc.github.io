An Fhideag Airgid
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Donald Shaw & Karen Matheson
Appears On : The Dreaming Sea
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " An Fhideag Airgid " on Capercaillie's album Glenfinnan

" The Silver Whistle " on Sìleas' album Beating Harps
