Sheáin Bháin
   
Credits: Traditional
   
Appears On: Cathie Ryan
   
Language: Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Seán Bháin" on Dervish's album Midsummer's Night
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Nach deas an baile an baile seo Isn't this a lovely town?
Nach deas an baile é Maoras Isn't Maoras a lovely town?
Nach deas an baile chuile baile Isn't every town a lovely town?
Ach Claidhneach glas na bhfaochain And grassy Clynagh of the periwinkle

Is tabhair sa gcoca féir mé Take me to the haystack

Mo mhíle slán go deo leat My fond farewell forever
Nach deas mar chuirfinn fataí dhuit How well I'd sow potatoes for you
Níos fearr ná bhainfinn móin dhuit Even better than I'd cut the turf for you
