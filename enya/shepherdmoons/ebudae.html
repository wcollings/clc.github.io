Ebudæ
Credits : Enya; lyrics by Roma Ryan
Appears On : Shepherd Moons ; Paint The Sky With Stars
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
Amharc, mná ag obair lá 's mall san oích Look, women working by day and late at night
Ceolann siad ar laetha geal, a bhí They sing of bright days that were
Bealach fada anonn 's anall a choích A long way back and forth forever
