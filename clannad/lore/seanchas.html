Seanchas
Credits : Ciarán Brennan
Appears On : Lore
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
An seanchas gearr, an seanchas is fearr The shortest wisdom is the best wisdom
An scéal is giorra is milse i mbéal The shortest story is sweetest in the mouth
An chuid is lú an chuid is fearr The smallest part is the best part
Focal nó dhó má thaithníonn lig dó A word or two if practicing allows it
