Gura Mise Tha Fo Mhulad 'S Mi Air Tulaich Na Buaile
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Mackenzie

Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)

Gura mise tha fo mhulad I am full of sorrow
