Gur E Mis' Tha Gu Tinn
   
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Maeve MacKinnon
   
Appears On : Gaelic Scotland (compilation)
   
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions : " Gaol Ise Gaol I " on Flora MacNeil's album Orain Floraidh
   

Lyrics : English Translation :
   
Gur e mis' tha gu tinn I am very unwell
   
Sèist : Chorus (after each verse) :
E ho hù o hù o E ho hù o hù o

Chan e goirteas mo chìnn It is not my head that feels the pain
   
Ach na chuir mi dhan chìll But the one I buried
   
Gaol ise 's luaidh i My love, my beloved
   
Mi cuimir 's mi cruinn I am bonnie, I am plump
   
'S mi gu biorshùileach bìnn I, with the sweet, bright eye
   
Mar chuthaig air craoibh Like a cuckoo in a tree
   
Mar smeòrach air bèinn Like a thrush on the hill
   
Mi dualach 's mi donn With my long, curly locks
