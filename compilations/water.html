The Water Is Wide
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Steeleye Span
   
Appears On: Voices Of Celtic Women 1 (compilation)
   
Language: English
   
Other Versions: "The Water Is Wide" on Mary Black's album Without The Fanfare
"The Water Is Wide" on Órla Fallon's album The Water Is Wide
   

Lyrics:

There is a ship that sails the sea
It is loaded deep as deep can be
But not so deep as the love I'm in
That I know not how, I sink or swim

The water is wide, we cannot get o'er
And neither have I wings to fly
Give me a boat that will carry two
That boat shall row my love and I
My love, my love and I
