Farewell Farewell
   
Credits: Richard Thompson; arranged by Danú
   
Appears On: The Road Less Traveled
   
Language: English
   
Other Versions: "Farewell Farewell" on Mary Black's album By The Time It Gets Dark
   

Lyrics:

Farewell, farewell to you who would hear
You lonely travellers all
The cold north wind will blow again
The winding road does call

And will you never return to see
Your bruised and beaten sons?
Oh I would, I would, if welcome I were
For they loath me, everyone

And will you never cut the cloth
Or drink the light to be?
And can you never swear a year
To anyone but we?

No, I never cut the cloth
Or drink the light to be
But I'll swear a year to one who lies
Asleep alongside of me

You lonely travellers all
