Éirigh Suas A Stóirín
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Karan Casey & Robbie Overson
Appears On : The Winds Begin To Sing
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " Éirigh Suas A Stóirín " on Moya Brennan's album Misty Eyed Adventures
" Éirigh Suas A Stóirín " on Clannad's album Dúlamán
" Éirigh Suas A Stóirín " on Mairéad Ní Dhomhnaill's album Mairéad Ní Dhomhnaill
