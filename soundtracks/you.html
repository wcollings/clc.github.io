You Raise Me Up
   
Credits: R. Loveland & B. Graham
   
Appears On: Celtic Woman (soundtrack)
   
Language: English
   
Other Versions: "You Raise Me Up" on Secret Garden's album Once In A Red Moon

I am strong when I am on your shoulders
