Aria
Credits : Rolf Lovland; lyrics by Mick O'Brien
Appears On : Dawn Of A New Century
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)

Lyrics : English Translation :
Molaim duit ó lá go lá I praise you day by day
Athair ar neamh, slánaithneoir Father in heaven, our savior
Saol ré órga imigh nainn The golden era leaves us now
Caoineadh is uaigneas ar mo chroíse Sadness and lonliness on my heart
Athas is gliondar ar mo smaointe Happiness and delight on my thoughts
An saol úr ag bun na speíre A new season on the horizon
Fada an lá, fada an óiche Long is the day, long is the night
Mol an óige is tiocfaidh sí slán Praise youth and it will always be there
