Mo Ghrá Thú (Salm 17)
   
Credits : Liam Lawson; arranged by Aoife Ní Fhearraigh, Máire Brennan & Denis Woods
   
Appears On : Aoife
   
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics : English Translation :
   
Curfá : Chorus :
Mo ghrá Thú, a Thiarna I love You, oh Lord
Mo neart Thú You are my strength
Mo ghrá Thú, a Thiarna I love You, oh Lord
   
Mo Dhia Thú mo charraig is mo dhídean You are my God, my rock and my shelter
Mo sciath, adharc mo shlánaithe, mo dhún My shield, ark of my salvation, my strength
Glaoim ar an Tiarna dar dIeacht moladh I implore the Lord worthy of all praise
Agus déarta mo shaoradh ó mo naimhde And I'd like to see myself safe from my enemies
   
(Curfá) (Chorus)
   
Go maire an Tiarna, is é mo charraig É May the Lord prosper, He is my rock
Go raibh Dia mo Shlánaitheoir á mhóradh May God be adored, my Savior
Thug sé caithréim an dá Rí He gave victory to the two Kings
Agus nocht sé a ghrá dá ungthach And showed His love to those He anointed
   
(Curfá) (Chorus)
