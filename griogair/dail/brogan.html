Nò`ogan Dannsaidh

Appears On : Dail-Riata
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
Lyrics: English Translation:
Còogan dannsaidh, The dancing shoes
an gnothach idir idir dhomh, won't do at all, at all
'òogan dannsaidh The dancing shoes are no use
Nàaireadh idir dhomh-sa the wouldn't last me at all
Nò`ogan fhuair mi an Dun-Phris The shoes I got in Dumfries
Gò`oidheach piollach although they were lovely and unusual looking
thug mi 'n charraig iad gam milleadh, I took to the rocks with them and spoiled them
'òoiceadh and that tore them to bits
gun robh 'n gutta-percha dair dhomh and gutta-percha was expensive for me
'S shaoil mi e' bhith saor ri cheannach and I thought it would be cheap
Thug e na builg air air mo bhuinn it gave me blisters on my soles
'ò`o air and I'll never forget it
Dè`eirich an t-uachdar on bhonn The upper came away from the sole
'à`alach and my big toe was sticking out
Sà`ailtean the heels split from the backs
'òomhla and they both fell off me
Cà`ach I'll really stand out
gà`al na banais if I go to the wedding dance
Bèeirr dhomh 'n dibhearsain a chall I'd prefer to miss out on all the fun
Nòogan than dance with rubbish shoes
Bèeitheadh They were of no use on the moor
'S cha robh feum annt anns a charraid and they were no use when I got cold and tired
'ìiosaich I would have to stay away from the fire
Gò`odhte although my beard would be frozen
Aè`eile 's tus iad the man who put them together in the first place
Cha toir mise turn dha tuilleadh I'll never give him a turn again
On a mheall e mi le foill Since he decieved me with his treachery
Gur ann a thoill e chorcach he deserves bad luck
Mòoigheann If he doesn't change his ways
Cò`o dha cuirean no-one will give him a turn again
Fùunadh he can just shut the door
'ù`uil na monadh and lie out at the back of the moor
'ì`in the money that I had saved up
Aì`ioball anns gach baile selling bibles in every village
'ùu my reputation is spoiled for
Aòoigean having considered the botcher useful
