The Boar's Head Carol
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Katie McMahon
Appears On : Celtic Christmas
Language : English & Latin

Lyrics : English Translation :
The boar's head in hand bear I
Bedecked with bay and rosemary
Pray you, my masters, be merry
Quot estis in convivio As you all feast so heartily
Chorus (after each verse) :
Caput apri defero Lo behold the head I bring
Redens laudes Domino Giving praise to God we sing

The boar's head, as I understand
Is the bravest dish in all the land
Thus bedecked with a gay garland
