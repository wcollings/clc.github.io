Fire
Credits : Adapted from the song "Byrd One Breara"; lyrics by Katie McMahon
Appears On : Shine
Language : English

Lyrics :

Silent in the starlight, mourning at the moonlight
Weeping black winds tear my face
We were one in the night, our souls turned to light
Where are you now, my heart's life?
Torn from my arms

Golden were the nights, the nights spent in the great hall
Ruby gems were glowing in the embers
Love, the flame that burned 'til all was pure light
You were the flower of the fire
But now all is cold
