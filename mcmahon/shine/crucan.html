Crucán Na bPáiste
   
Credits : Traditional music Cailín Na Gruaige Báinne adapted by Brendan Graham; lyrics by Brendan Graham
   
Appears On : Shine ; Gaelic Ireland (compilation)
   
Language : Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions : " Crucan Na bPaiste " on Karen Matheson's album Downriver
