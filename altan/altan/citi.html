Cití Na gCumann
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Frankie Kennedy, Mairéad Ní Mhaonaigh, Mark Kelly & Ciarán Curran
   
Appears On: Altan
   
Language: Gaeilge (Irish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Cití Na gCumann" on Máire Brennan's single Jealous Heart
