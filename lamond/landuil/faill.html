Fàill Ìll Ò Ro
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Mary Jane Lamond, Geoff Arsenault, Luke Morris, Brad Davidge, Wendy MacIsaac, Cathy Porter & Phil Strong
   
Appears On: Làn Dùil
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Gur mise tha trom, airtneulach I am gloomy and fatigued
'S a' mhadainn 's mi ag éiridh In the morning as I rise
Tha 'ghaoth an ear a' gobachadh The wind that is working around
'S cha n-i mo thogairt fhéin i From the east is not my desire
