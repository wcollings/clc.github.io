Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you have the lyrics to the song ________?
A: If you don't see the lyrics on this site, try the various sites on the Sources & Links page or Google . You can also try posting your request on the Lyrics Corner Forum . If you can't find them there, then chances are, the answer is no.

Q: Can you translate the song ________ for me?
A: The only languages I speak are English, Spanish, and some German, so if the original lyrics are in those languages, I can probably help you - but in that case, a translation should already be on this site. If the lyrics are in any other language and there is no translation already on the site, sorry, but I can't help you.

Q: Why don't you have the group/artist ________ on your site?
A: I only list CDs that I own on this site. The reason for this is because, when adding new pages, I always listen to the CDs to make sure that the lyrics are as accurate as possible. If you don't see a group or artist listed, most likely I don't have any of their CDs.

Q: You're missing ________'s album ________!
A: See the previous answer. None of the artists' pages are intended to be complete discographies by any means.

Q: I can send you the lyrics to ________ if you want to add them.
A: Again, see above. I will not add lyrics if I don't already have the CD in question. Of course, corrections to lyrics already on the site are always welcome.

Q: Do you have sheet music/guitar tabs?
A: Unfortunately, no.

Q: Can I put a link to your website on my page?
A: By all means, just please let me know first!

Q: Can you put a link to my page on your website?
A: Depends on the link! It obviously must be Celtic music-related, preferably lyrics-related. Send it along and I'll let you know!

Feel free to drop me a line if you have a question not answered here.
