Appears On : Gach Sgeul
Language : Gàidhlig
Lyrics: English Translation:
An Ròn The Seal
"Mise nighean Rìgh-fo-Thuinn "I am daughter of the King-under-Sea
Fuil nan rìghrean na mo chrè - Royal blood flows in my veins -
Ged a chì sibh mi nam ròn Though you see me as a seal
Tha mi mòrail nam thìr fhèin. I am noble in my own land.
   
"Tìr-fo-Thuinn mo dhachaigh dhùint' "Land-below-waves my prison home,
Innis dhùthchasach nan ròn; Hereditary domain of the seal;
Caidlidh mi air leacan sàil', I will sleep on a salt sea slab,
Mi fhìn 's mo bhàn-chuilean òg." Myself and my white-furred pup."
   
A Bhana-phrionns' a' chuain shiar, O Princess of the western ocean
A bheil sgeul agad ri luaidh? Do you have a tale to weave?
Nach inns thu dhuinn mar a bha Will you tell us how it was
Mun do ghabh sibh tàmh sa chuan? Before you came to live at sea?
   
"Chaidh na geasan a chur oirnn "Spells were laid upon us
Rè ar beò-bhith le luchd-fuath, During our human lives by foes -
'S ged a tha sinn snàmh nan caol Though we now swim the straits
'S e nàdar daonnd' tha dhuinn dual. Human nature is our heritage.
   
"Aig tràth-marbh air oidhche fèill "At the dead of feast-day night
Tilgidh sinn ar bèin air tràigh, We cast our sealskins on the sand,
'S cluichidh sinn nar n-òighean suairc' Playing there as gentle maids
A' crathadh ar cuaillean bàn. Shaking our blonde tresses.
   
"Ach a-nochd tha mi nam ròn "But tonight I am a seal
Air an lic an còrs' a' chuain: On a rock beside the sea;
'S e mo nàdar bhith toirt gaol, It is my nature to give love,
'S do chlann-daoine thug mi luaidh." And mankind I hold dear."

is far nach cluinnte guth duin' ach fuaim tuinne 's glog geòidh where a human voice was never heard, only the sound of the waves and the calling of geese
