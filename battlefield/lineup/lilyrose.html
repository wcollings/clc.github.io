The Lily and the Rose
   
   
Appears On: Line-Up
   
Language: English
   

Lyrics:
As I walked out,
one evening so clear,
a young man lamented the loss of his dear
And as he lamented,
full sore did he cry,
"Alas I'm tormented, for love must I die!"

"My dear and my jewel,
my honey" says he,
"would you let me gang wi' you,
your sweetheart to be?
My dear and my jewel,
my honey" said he,
"would you let me gang wi' you,
your true love to be?"

"Were I to say yes,
I'd speak against my mind,
for to say no, you'd think me unkind.
To sit and say nothin', you'd say I was dumb.
Take that for your answer, and go as you come!"

"A pox on you, Sally,
for you are unkind!
You took the lily, left the red rose behind,
But the lily will yellow,
and the time will come soon,
when the red rose will flourish
in the sweet month of June"

Some marry for beauty,
but beauty soon fades,
some marry for riches,
get wild, [?] glades,
but if I should marry,
and plain you may see,
the wee lass that's loyal
is the darling for me.
