MhicShiridh
Credits : Traditional; arranged by Alyth McCormack & Jim Sutherland
Appears On : An Iomall
Language : Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
Other Versions : " Chan E Caoidh Mhic Shiridh " on Maggie MacInnes' album Spiorad Beatha
" Chan E Caoidh Mhic Shiridh " on Flora MacNeil's album Craobh Nan Ubhal

Lyrics : English Translation :
Chan e caoidh Mhic Shiridh 'Tis not lamenting o'er MhicShiridh
A dh'fhàg an dil air mo ghruaidh That on my cheeks the tears remain
