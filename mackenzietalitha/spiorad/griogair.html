Griogair
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Talitha MacKenzie
   
Appears On: Spiorad
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Griogal Cridhe" on Ishbel MacAskill's album Essentially Ishbel
"Griogal Cridhe" on Fiona Mackenzie & Arthur Cormack's album Seinn! O Ho Ro Seinn!
"Griogal Cridhe" on Mac-talla's album ...Mairidh Gaol Is Ceol
"Beloved Gregor" on Whistlebinkies' album Inner Sound
   

Lyrics: English Translation:
   
Ged bhiodh ann cur is cathadh Even in the drifting snow
Is sìde nan seachd sian With all seven elements abounding
Gheibheadh Griogair dhomsa cnacan Gregor would find a crevice for me
'San caidlimid fo dhìon And we would sleep in its shelter

Is mór a b'annsa bhith aig Griogair I would rather be with Gregor
Fo bhrata ruibeach ròin Under a hairy sealskin rug
Na bhith aig Baran crìon na Dalach Than with the withered Baron of Dal
Ag giùlan sìoda is sròil With silk and satin to wear

Nam biodh agamsa an sin cupan If I had had a wee cup there
Dh'òlainn dith mo shàth I would have drunk my fill

A' bualadh mo dhà làimh Beating with my two hands
