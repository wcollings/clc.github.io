Saor An t-Sàbhaidh
   
Credits: Traditional; arranged by Talitha MacKenzie & Chris Birkett
   
Appears On: Spiorad
   
Language: Gàidhlig (Scottish Gaelic)
   
Other Versions: "Puirt A Beul" on Mairi MacInnes' album This Feeling Inside
"O Ho Na Ribeannan" on Tannas' album Rù-Rà
